Welcome to the Sheffield Ruby User Group site. We meet on the second Monday of each month to discuss and learn about Ruby, Rails and other interesting tech/web/programming stuff.

If you're new to Ruby or want to learn more about Rails then drop in, or if you've got something to share and want to give a talk then let us know!

We also organise the occasional event or workshop.

Follow us on Twitter @sheffieldruby

Where do you meet?

With the help of epiGenesys, we hold our meetings at the Enterprise Zone.
