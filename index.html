Welcome to the Sheffield Ruby User Group.

Who are we?

We're a group of people who are interested in Ruby, Rails and other tech/web/programming stuff.

If you're new to Ruby or want to learn more about Rails then feel free to drop in.

If you've got something to share and want to give a talk then let us know!

When and where do we meet?

We meet on the second Monday of each month at {{ site.default_location }}.

After each meeting we usually go to The Red Deer for a drink, some nibbles and a chat.
