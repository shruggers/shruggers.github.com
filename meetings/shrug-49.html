ShRUG festive meal

Date:

Location:

It’s that time of the year again. Time to put the talks and workshops to one side for a month and get together for ShRUG’s annual Christmas meal.

This year we’re going to Ginseng Korean Restaurant for 7pm.

We’ll be booking a table, so be sure to let us know that you’re coming so we have confirmed numbers by responding on the Google+ Page or dropping us an email or Tweet us.
